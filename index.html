Features

Everything your crew needs to actually decide.

Plan-IT replaces the 200-message group chat with a flow that always ends in a real plan: propose, vote, break ties, go — and remember it after.

✦

Nova AI concierge

Nova notices when your crew is free, nudges you to start a plan, and can pick for you when nobody can decide. Feeling spontaneous? Hit Wing-It.

▦

Real-time voting

Drop in options, share a join code, and watch votes roll in live. Everyone gets a say — and the leading option is always clear.

◎

Tiebreaker spin wheel

Deadlocked? Send it to the wheel. A fully animated spinner settles ties in seconds, and nobody can argue with fate.

≡

Itineraries that stick

Once a plan is finalized, RSVPs lock and a stop-by-stop itinerary goes live — pregame, main event, and the late-night pizza run.

▣

Memories

After the night ends, a 24-hour window opens for everyone to drop photos. Grid, stories, or slideshow — your night, captured together.

◔

Connections & tiers

Grow your network from your origin invite, earn XP for every plan, and climb the tiers from Newcomer all the way to Legend.

How it works

Three steps. Zero chaos.

From “what should we do?” to walking out the door.

STEP 01

Start a plan

Name it, add a few options — wine bar, tacos, movie night — and share the join code with your crew.

STEP 02

Vote it out

Everyone votes in real time. Tied? The spin wheel makes the call. The winner is announced with confetti, obviously.

STEP 03

Go, then relive it

The itinerary goes live with times and stops. Afterwards, Memories opens so everyone can share photos from the night.
